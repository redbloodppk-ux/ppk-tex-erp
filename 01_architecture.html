📐 The Math

Two cost numbers, not one

The single most important idea in this ERP: separate what you charge from what it really costs you.

◆ Quoted Cost
What customers see
Warp + Weft + Pick (market) +
Bobbin + Porvai + Commissions

Used for quotations, price lists, sales invoices. Pick paise reflects market rate, not your real overhead.

★ True Cost
What it really costs
Warp + Weft + LOOMS overhead +
Bobbin + Porvai + Commissions

Used for stock valuation and real P&L. Includes ₹5.14/mtr of factory overhead.

The Pricing Gap = Quoted − True
Positive = safe margin · Negative = under-priced (the ERP flashes red so you can decide: raise price, route to vendor, or discontinue)
